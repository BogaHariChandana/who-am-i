CLASSICAL DANCER

Several prominent classical dancers have significantly contributed to Indian dance forms. These include figures like Birju Maharaj (Kathak), Rukmini Devi Arundale (Bharatanatyam), Kelucharan Mohapatra (Odissi), Guru Bipin Singh (Manipuri), and Guru Vempati Chinna Satyam (Kuchipudi), according to a blog post on Indian classical dance. Other notable dancers include Yamini Krishnamurthy, Padma Subrahmanyam, and Alarmel Valli, notes a website about Indian dance.

ARTISTS

A few famous artists from history include Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Pablo Picasso. In India, Raja Ravi Varma is renowned for his blend of European academic realism with Indian subjects. Other notable Indian artists include Rabindranath Tagore and Jamini Roy.

MUSICIANS

Classical Music Maestros: Ravi Shankar: A legendary sitarist who popularized Indian classical music in the West. Zakir Hussain: A tabla virtuoso celebrated for his mastery and innovative approach to the instrument. Hariprasad Chaurasia: A renowned flautist known for his mastery of the bansuri. Bismillah Khan: A shehnai maestro who brought the instrument to prominence. Shivkumar Sharma: A santoor player who popularized the instrument in Indian classical music. L. Subramaniam: A violinist known for his versatility and contributions to both classical and fusion music.
